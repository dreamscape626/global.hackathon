Save the Forests

A game to stop C02 emissions from spreading before time runs out.


Instructions: Once you press "Start," the timer begins.


The goal here is to stop the forests from disappearing by planting trees, before the year 2114.


Over time, deforestation destroys the trees that store carbon, thereby increasing the amount of carbon into the atmosphere. Excessive carbon in the atmosphere leads to global warming, drier climates, declines in habitat, disruption to biodiversity, and is generally bad for everyone living on Earth.


At the rate we're going, we'll run out of forests in 100 years. This game is intended to bring awareness to the importance of mitigating the effects of deforestation before it's too late.
